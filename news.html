Milestone

Milestone

1997

The building established in 1997 as foreign investment company in Indonesia. Our location is at Jalan Bangil - Sukorejo KM. 04 Desa Oro-Oro Ombo Wetan, Kecamatan Rembang, Kabupaten Pasuruan, East Java, Indonesia. It took only 1 hour from the second bug city in Indonesia, Surabaya.

2011

Indonesia Fertilizer Supplier founded in 2011. The South Korean investor and fertilizer expert bring their technology to produce high quality fertilizer in Indonesia and the company starter exporting its products to Korea.

2015

By 2015, the company is acquired by local investors and combines knowledge and technology from Korea with trained human and management resources from Indonesia to improve effectiveness and efficiency in producing high quality and better products. This factory supplies all their product to fulfil organic fertilizer demand in Korea.

2017

In 2017, we began to expand the market to local and overseas beside Korea. We add more variations on product types as well.

Our vision is to be the best organic fertilizer producer in Indonesia which is supply for high quality, effective, efisien and eco-friendly. What make us different with another company in Indonesia, we have organic matter, NPK, which is 45-50% organic matter.
